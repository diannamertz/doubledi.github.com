Heard Here

Project Type:

iOS for iPad and iPhone

Year Completed:

In progress

About:

Heard Here is a music player journal that logs where and when you listen to songs so that you can create playlists from a particularly magical moment or location. Just use the search options to filter your songs, and follow along on the map view of your song's original location.
