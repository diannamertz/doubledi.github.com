Heard Here

Project Type:

iOS for iPad and iPhone

Year Completed:

In progress

About:

Heard Here is a music player journal that logs where and when you listen to songs so that you can create playlists from a particularly magical moment or location. Just use the search options to filter your songs, and follow along on the map view of your song's original location.
Heard Here provides the following features:
  • Logs time, location, and song details from all music players on the device
  • Provides user-defined playlists based on time and location filters
  • Supplies an in-app music player
  • Illustrates the time and location of each song's original play
Heard Here is a native iOS app for iPhone and iPad, programmed in Objective C

In its current state, Heard Here is semi-functional. At present, the user can select songs from the playlists and use the music player controls: play music, skip forward, skip backward, and adjust volume. The custom UI is integrated, although final adjustments are inevitable. More to come!
