Appropriator

Project Type:

iOS for iPhone

Year Completed:

2013

About:

Create your own masterpiece! Appropriator provides a library of selected elements from famous paintings throughout history. Snap a photo or import an image and embellish it with one or more painting elements. The elements are moveable, scalable, rotatable, and flippable so that they fit perfectly into your image. Share the resulting masterpiece with friends!
