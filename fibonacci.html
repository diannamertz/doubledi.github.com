The Fibonacci Sequence

Project Type

Year Completed

About

This experiment visualizes the relationship between the Fibonacci numbers and the Fibonacci spirals in a pine cone. The seeds of a pine cone form two sets of Fibonacci spirals in opposite directions that originate in the base of a pine cone. The number of spirals in each direction count up to a Fibonacci number: 8 and 13.
